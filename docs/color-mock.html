Atelier · palettes pulled from references, not other UIs

Dark is “Aegean Dusk” — a cool petrol-teal foundation with the sun's gold accent, sampled from a sunset over water. Light is “Studio” — sampled from a watercolor of a painter's atelier: warm parchment paper, oxblood-brown ink, delft-blue primary, honey-gold signal, ivy green. Across both, gold stays the rare “agent / live” accent and green & red stay reserved for data (credit/debit, reconciled/break).
